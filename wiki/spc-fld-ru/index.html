Object Class: Safe Keter

Description: A caucasian white male person with dark hair, who has been found in the depths of Donbas in Donetsk People's Republic. Specimen doesn't seem to attack anyone in its range, however it will sometimes create and send disturbing mediafiles though various means of communication of the Internet.
